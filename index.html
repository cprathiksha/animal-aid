Save Endangered Animals

FEATURED PROJECTS

Worldwide, 90% of all living creatures are on the brink of extinction. You can help them.

There are many things we can do to help endangered animals, here are a few suggestions.
Protect wildlife habitats. Habitat loss is one of the biggest causes of extinction.
Educate others.
Stay away from pesticides and herbicides.
Shop ethically.
Be an ethical citizen, tourist and when you see endangered animals threatened let someone know immediately. Or email us and we will try to help contact the appropriate agencies or individuals.
